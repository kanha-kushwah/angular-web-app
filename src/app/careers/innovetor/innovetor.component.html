We are building a dream team of entrepreneurs who can create, execute and deliver value with impact.

Who is a Creative Innovator ? …and why are we looking for them ?

The creative innovators are a special breed of people who have this very rare combination of imaginative free thinking and a logical engineering mind. The creatives question the next big idea; and the innovators execute structured value driven outputs of an existing idea.

  1. 01 Generating new ideas
  2. 02Creating workable blue-prints of the ideas
  3. 03Ability to communicate and convince your team of your ideas
  4. 04Be a super specialist in at least two skills
  5. 05Be a motivating and inspiring team member
  6. 06Be an entrepreneur at heart
  7. 07 Always have a combination of objectivity and compassion in decision making

There is no textbook explanation of how to become a Creative Innovator. The points mentioned above is LE’s standard of identifying its breed of people.
